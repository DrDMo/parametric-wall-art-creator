Parametric Wall Art Creator

Install by copying the single folder named Parametric Wall Art Creator into your Vectric Gadgets folder, then restart VCarve/Aspire.

Expected structure:
Parametric Wall Art Creator/
  Parametric Wall Art Creator.lua
  INSTALL.txt

Notes:
- This edition runs in either inch-based or millimeter-based jobs.
- The gadget follows the active Vectric job units automatically.
- Default clamp margin remains 0.75 in / 19.05 mm on all sheet edges.
- The gadget enforces the clamp margin as a minimum, even if a smaller value is entered.
- Generate Toolpaths becomes available after Generate Geometry has been run in the current session.
